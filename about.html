About Vince

Four decades of excellence in marine and automotive detailing

My Story

Since 1982, Vince's Marine Detailing has been the trusted name in Orange County for premium boat and automotive detailing services. What started as a hobby has grown into a legacy of excellence, serving countless satisfied customers throughout Southern California.

With over 40 years of experience, I've developed specialized techniques and use only the finest products to ensure your vessel or vehicle receives the care it deserves.
